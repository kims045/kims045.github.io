8. Classic car mania



Cadillac Motor Car Division, is one of the biggest automobile manufacturer, one of the most luxury vehicle company, and Cadillac-branded vehicles are mostly distributed in United States, Canada, and China, but also Cadillac-branded vehicles are distributed in 34 additional markets worldwide. Historically, Cadillac automobiles have always won the top of the luxury field within the United States.

Cadillac is among the oldest automobile brands in the world, which means a lot of classic car is from Cadillac. Also, Cadillac was the first American car to win the Royal Automobile Club of the United Kingdom's Dewar Trophy by successfully demonstrating the interchangeability of its component parts during a reliability test in 1908; this spawned the firm's slogan "Standard of the World". It won the trophy again in 1912 for incorporating electric starting and lighting in a production automobile. Throughout history, the Cadillac brand has played a significant role in escorting our presidents safely and stylishly.

Cadillac also created world’s first concept car which was designed by Harley Earl in 1938. It’s design includes debut of retractable headlamps, power windows, power convertible top and integrated side panels.
